Welcome to your Memo!

Memo project is an easy day by day note application where you can write anything that you want. It's your agenda, your diary, your secretary and anything you can imagine!

Start your memo today, choose from the dates widget another date and continue making your memos!

Memo project is based on simplicity and flexibility. Only you can see your Memo, privacy is a priority for Memo.

Memo is still a Prototype, but in a future you will be able to:

  • See a calendar where days with notes will be marked with a relevant text about that day.
  • Attach files to a day.
  • Search notes by keywords like Google style.
  • Download your memories from date to date range.
  • Login with your Facebook or Twitter account.
  • And much more! You are the best person to tell us what you want in Memo project, feel free to write to us to webmaster@memo.do
